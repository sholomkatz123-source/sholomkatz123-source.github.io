Programs

Structured, uplifting time — after school and at night

From homework clubs to sports and skill‑building labs, our programs channel energy into growth and healthy social life.

Ages 9–12

Homework Club

Quiet, focused support with mentors on hand to guide study skills and keep kids on track.

Ages 12–15

Study & Skill Labs

Hands‑on workshops in coding, design, music, and more — discover strengths and build confidence.

Ages 13–17

Night League

Sports and teamwork in a supervised, positive environment that forges friendships and resilience.

All Ages

Community Service

Service projects that give back — turning free time into purpose and leadership.

High School

Mentor Track

Train older teens to lead younger groups, model good choices, and earn volunteer credits.

Weeknights

Open Space

A safe, supervised hangout with games, snacks, and space to unwind — with positive boundaries.

Impact

Where time is guided, potential grows

We partner with families to create safe rhythms that keep students engaged, social, and moving toward their best.

Outcomes

  • Improved homework completion & study habits
  • Healthy peer networks & positive role models
  • Reduced unstructured screen time
  • Confidence through skill‑building and service

Safeguards

  • Trained, vetted staff & volunteers
  • Clear conduct expectations
  • Supervision and check‑in/out procedures
  • Spaces designed for safety and belonging

Partnerships

We collaborate with parents, schools, and community leaders to align support around each student.
